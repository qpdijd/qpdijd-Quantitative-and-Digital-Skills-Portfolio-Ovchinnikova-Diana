 Project 1 – "Investment Decision Analysis"
 This project analyzes weekly stock returns and risk measures using statistical methods.

Skills Developed:
During this exercise, several important analytical and statistical skills were developed.
The task involved collecting and organizing financial return data, calculating descriptive statistics, and interpreting measures
such as mean return, variance, standard deviation, and interquartile range to compare investment risk and performance.
In addition, covariance and correlation were calculated to evaluate the relationship between two stocks. 
A regression analysis was also performed to examine the relationship between market returns and stock performance using the S&P 500 index.
The exercise further involved applying financial metrics such as the Sharpe ratio and understanding statistical testing methods.

Overall, the assignment strengthened skills in financial data analysis, statistical interpretation, regression modeling, and the use of spreadsheet tools for quantitative analysis.
